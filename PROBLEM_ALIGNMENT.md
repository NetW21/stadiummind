## 🏟️ Azteca Stadium Matchday Scenarios Supported

The platform is designed to align with real tournament operation scenarios for the FIFA World Cup 2026:

### 1. Crowd Management & Ingress Optimization
* **Real-world Problem**: Bottlenecks at Gate A due to digital reader outages cause extreme queue build-ups.
* **Our Solution**: The **Crowd Intelligence** view monitors wait times and uses Gemini to formulate dynamic rerouting strategies to Gate B/C.

### 2. Transportation & Fleet Management
* **Real-world Problem**: Rapid arrivals at Metro stations cause heavy passenger surges, overloading transport loops.
* **Our Solution**: The **Transportation Hub** monitors capacity factors and allows real-time adjustments of intervals (e.g., shortening loops by 2 mins).

### 3. Emergency Coordination
* **Real-world Problem**: Stadium emergencies require rapid first-responder corridors and staff dispatch.
* **Our Solution**: The **Emergency Centre** maps incident severity and assigns multilingual volunteers based on skills (e.g. assigning Spanish-speaking volunteers to Gate A).

### 4. Accessibility Accommodation
* **Real-world Problem**: Visually impaired or wheel-chaired fans experience difficulty navigating steps or steep stairs.
* **Our Solution**: The **Accessibility Assist** panel plans barrier-free routing and provides text-scaling features.
